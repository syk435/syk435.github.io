About Tristar

Tristar Technologies has developed algorithms to analyze the body and human behavior, not replicated anywhere else in the industry. Our technology uses a combination of regular RGB camera footage with AI-based software to deliver actionable information to commercial customers. We have an obsession with real-time performance, minimizing false alerts, intuitive and beautiful user interfaces, and creating powerful AI models. Our company has rich experience in the manufacturing industry, and a strong network of trusted advisors and customers.

Leadership

Tristar was founded in 2018 by Harvard and MIT alumni to build a computer vision and AI platform for analyzing human kinematics and imagery. Tristar has previously created similar solutions for plastic surgery clinics, Colorado Children’s Hospital, MLB player analysis, and facial recognition for homeless outreach.
