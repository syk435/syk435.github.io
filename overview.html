Everything you need to boost process

Edge Computing

Our smart camera device can be placed wherever you have the need for observing processes in your facility. Built on the latest technology, it contains state-of-the-art NVIDIA hardware capable of on-site computer vision and AI analytics at lightning speed. We don't depend on using existing CCTV footage like the other guys - we prioritize company privacy and the freedom to easily monitor any section of your plant and make changes at any time.

AI Pose Estimation and Tracking

With our proprietary AI pose estimation and tracking models, you can define a worker task and gather information on how well an employee carries it out, if they are where they're supposed to be, and what tasks they do throughout their shift. Our intelligent system observes the kinematics of a person's body and analyzes how they can perform better. Using the latest and greatest algorithms our team of MIT and Harvard engineers have built best-in-class software models for Tristar's platform.

Dashboard and Alerts

The LUM dashboard sends automatic alerts anytime an issue comes up on the manufacturing floor. Get live updates on all employees in a department and view live footage. A profile and performance history is compiled for every employee, allowing you to gauge how well they are performing and where to improve. Get the data you need on your workforce to plan and optimize day-to-day operations - reducing costs and saving time.

Analytics

View advanced analytics on your overall workforce within the LUM Dashboard. View information on each task - from time wasted by employees to which employee works fastest. Receive an eagle's eye perspective of which areas on the plant floor are the most error-prone, which areas have missed tasks and need extra staffing, and see how well each shift works together to get things done.

Predictions

The LUM Dashboard includes predictive models that analyze the performance of your plant and give suggestions to streamline workflows and reduce cost. State-of-the art forecasting techniques tell you what changes to make - from reassigning employees, to replacing machinery that require too many repairs. Understand what changes you can make to reduce cost and increase efficiency like never before.
